Kentucky Bluegrass Region Springs


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Ethan Russell

This map shows Springs per 2.5 and 5 Mile Hexagonal grid in Kentucky in the bluegrass region and in the state. The CRS reference of EPSG: 3089 was used to project the map. The number of springs is shown in the legend with the corresponding color. Map prepared by Ethan Russell for Geology 409 March, 2018 Using QGIS 2.18.16 on Windows 10 Data acquired from Kentucky Divion of Water, groundwater springs layer. Locations of all the inventoried springs in the Commonwealth of Kentucky: ftp://data.gis.eppc.ky.gov/shapefiles/dow_groundwater_springs.zip Data aquired from the U.S. Census Bureau Geography Divion, TIGER/Line Shapefile Data aquired from the U.S. Census Dureau Cartographic Boundary Shapefiles, 1:500k Scale State and County Polygons.
