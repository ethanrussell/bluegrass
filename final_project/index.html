Sally Brown Crutcher Nature Preserve

Authored by: Ethan Russell

Sally Brown Crutcher Preserve consists of 759 acres of land located in Garrard County, Kentucky. Activities in the preserve include hiking and the option to explore the Kentucky River.

The park is somewhat hilly with very narrow dirt paths. It is acceptatble for anyone to hike the trail. Points of Intrest are marked on the map above. If vising during spring the "Crutcher Trail" is full of flowers.
